Our Story

One in five people in Hong Kong is living under the poverty line. Many
stay in cage homes and struggle to pay their monthly bill.

Unknowingly, there are many resources offered by NGO’s, tech
corporations, SME’s, and startups, such as donation, day care centers,
non-traditional courses, and workshops.

Therefore, Necessiti’s vision is to connect with the 600,000 families living
in poverty in Hong Kong with more than 800 NGO’s and tech-impact
solutions through one platform. Our objective is to empower families so
they can worry less about their financial situations and help unleash their
growth potential.

Why, What, Why

Core Values

We believe in social equality, community connectivity, and the impact which new technologies have.

Goal

Our goal is to alleviate poverty in Hong Kong through a platform of online services and resources.

Mission

Our mission is to help people in need meet their need through a reliable platform of free resources (donation centers, food/homeless shelters), employment opportunities via online skills training (coding schools, English lessons), and free events.

Our Solution

Currently, there is a lack of communication and clarity between what the
government, NGO's, startups, and corporations provide for the poor.

Our solution is to provide a one-stop platform that allows low-income
and at-risk families and individuals to easily browse and pick all resources
available to them according to their specific needs.
